Zegra Armed Directorate Forces (ZADF)

The Zegra Armed Directorate Forces (ZADF) serve as the principal military institution of the People’s Directorate of Zegra (PDZ). Directly loyal to Premier Antonovich Morozov, the ZADF functions both as a conventional military force and an instrument of political control. ZADF doctrine emphasizes domination over key terrain, urban sectors, and civilian population centers — prioritizing operational presence over winning hearts and minds.

The armed forces are structured around regionally raised directorate brigades, with a focus on rapid internal mobilization, counter-insurgency preparedness, and mechanized warfare.

Zegra Directorate Air Forces (ZDAF)

The Zegra Directorate Air Forces (ZDAF) are a heavily centralized air wing under direct command of the General Directorate Staff. The ZDAF operates primarily in support of ground forces and internal security operations. Like the ground forces, air power is doctrinally tied to the principle of population control and strategic denial of mobility to enemy formations.

During the beginning of the Zegra secession crisis, militias were able to storm relatively disorganized Russian airbases and capture a large amount of stored legacy airfames in addition to several more modern airframes. Recent efforts under the Zegra Technological Initiative aim to incorporate indigenous drone and EW (electronic warfare) capability into the air wing.
